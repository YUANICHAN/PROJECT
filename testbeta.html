In this inventory system, key operations include adding, updating, deleting, and archiving items. Adding involves inputting details and saving them. Updating modifies existing item information. Deleting removes items from the database, while archiving moves obsolete items to a separate section for future reference.

This inventory system tracks real-time product history and updates total products, total price, and total quantity. It provides accurate records for efficient inventory management and decision-making.
